  ABOUT PAGE
================================================================

  ---- Our Story ----

STORY HEADING: The Bar We Always Wanted
STORY PARAGRAPH 1: Stormy Monday is named after the blues standard — a song about the weight of Monday and the hope that the weekend brings. The name felt right for a bar that takes the craft seriously but doesn't take itself too seriously.
STORY PARAGRAPH 2: We took over 820 Alton Road, the longtime home of Macchialina, and turned it into something quieter and more personal. Fifty-five seats. Ten at the bar. Designed for conversations that run long, for rounds that arrive without being rushed.
STORY PARAGRAPH 3: The compass in our logo points in every direction — toward wherever you need to go, and toward the bar when you need to come back. Direction, movement, and return.
STORY PULL QUOTE: "This project is really about two Miami guys getting to build the bar we always wanted to hang out in."


  ---- Philosophy Panels (three columns below the story) ----

PHILOSOPHY 1 HEADING: The Drinks
PHILOSOPHY 1 BODY: Balanced and technique-driven. No smoke-filled cloches, no TikTok theatrics. We care about what's in your glass more than the production around it.

PHILOSOPHY 2 HEADING: The Food
PHILOSOPHY 2 BODY: Bar food from a Michelin kitchen. Chef Seth's snacks are built for drinking alongside — serious technique, relaxed format.

PHILOSOPHY 3 HEADING: The Music
PHILOSOPHY 3 BODY: Live every Monday and Friday. The playlist on other nights fits the room. We named the bar after a blues standard for a reason.


  ---- Team ----

TEAM MEMBER 1 NAME: James MacInnes
TEAM MEMBER 1 ROLE: Founder · Bar
TEAM MEMBER 1 BIO: A Cutler Bay native who has shaped some of Miami's most respected cocktail programs — KYU, Kaori, KLAW, and Wynwood's Shiso. With Stormy Monday, he built the bar he always wanted to hang out in.

TEAM MEMBER 2 NAME: Seth Blumenthal
TEAM MEMBER 2 ROLE: Chef
TEAM MEMBER 2 BIO: Chicago native, Miami-based for nearly a decade. Former chef de cuisine at Alter and at Le Jardinier during its Michelin-starred run. His food is technique-driven without being precious about it.

TEAM MEMBER 3 NAME: Jason Kipnis
TEAM MEMBER 3 ROLE: Partner
TEAM MEMBER 3 BIO: Former MLB All-Star. A partner in the project and the kind of guy who shows up to make things work.
